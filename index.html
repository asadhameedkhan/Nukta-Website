About Nukta

With a strong history of half a decade, Nukta is one of the biggest All-Pak declamations, dramatics and short films championships. This year, Nukta'16 will mark its 5th edition. With 600+ participants from over 60+ universities from all over Pakistan and with an audience of about 600+, Nukta has established a strong reputation among passionate students all over Pakistan. It is hosted and organized by the collaboration of two major societies at PIEAS; PIEAS Performing Arts Society (PPAS) and PIEAS Debating Society (PDS).

Modules of Nukta

Declamation

The bilingual declamation at NUKTA have gained an estimable position throughout Pakistan for hosting quality debates. All universities send their best speakers to compete at NUKTA for the championship.

Dramatics

The dramatics segment is undoubtedly one of the most captivating aspects of NUKTA. It has managed to unearth a huge chunk of hidden talent in Pakistan and provides the participants with the exposure they need to polish their skills. NUKTA has seen amazing performances from prestigious teams from institutions such as BNU, UOL, PU, GIKI and IST.

Short Films

A generous amount of time at NUKTA is dedicated to screening powerful short films and eloquent documentaries. It is common for the large audience to get overwhelmed and excited during our videography segments.
